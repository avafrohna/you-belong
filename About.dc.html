Our mission

Everyone deserves to feel at home in San Diego.

You Belong San Diego is a non-profit with one job: helping people who are new here find the communities, causes, and gatherings where they actually fit.

Browse the directory →

Why we built this

Moving to a new city is lonely in a way nobody warns you about. San Diego is full of welcoming people and good causes — but when you first arrive, you have no idea where any of it is.

So we made one trustworthy place to look. Every listing is added by hand and chosen because it's genuinely welcoming to newcomers. No pay-to-play, no endless feed — just the real thing.

What makes us different

Curated, not crowdsourced

A real person checks every listing. No spam, no dead links, no algorithm deciding what you see.

Genuinely local

From North County to South Bay — neighborhood-level picks, not the same five tourist spots.

Free & non-profit

A registered 501(c) non-profit. The directory is free to use and always will be.

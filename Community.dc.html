North Park Newcomers

A no-pressure weekly meetup for people who just moved to San Diego. Walk in a stranger, leave with a couple of plans.

About this group

North Park Newcomers started as a handful of transplants who were tired of doom-scrolling on their couches after work. Every Wednesday they grab a table at a neighborhood spot and welcome whoever shows up — no membership, no icebreaker games, no agenda beyond meeting people who also just got here.

It's the kind of low-stakes regular thing that turns a new city into a place with actual friends in it. Come once, come every week — both are normal.

What to expect

A friendly host who'll introduce you around so you're never standing alone.
A mix of people one week to six months into their San Diego move.
Zero cost and zero commitment — buy a drink if you want one, that's it.
